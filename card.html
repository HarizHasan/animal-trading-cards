Tiger shark

Objects found within the stomachs of captured tiger sharks include stingrays, squids, sea snakes, seals, birds, old tires...

  • Scientific Name: Galeocerdo cuvier
  • Average Length: 3.25-4.25 metres
  • Average Lifespan: 50 years
  • Habitat: Tropical/warm waters

A deadly and solitary hunter, tiger sharks are notable for having the most diverse diet out of any shark in the world, having a reputation as a "garbage disposal." They have sharp, serrated teeth and incredibly powerful jaws, which can be used to crack open the shells of clams and sea turtles.
